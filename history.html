Eloheh Health

“Caring for you, innovating for tomorrow”

Welcome! This page tells the story of how Eloheh Health grew from a small clinic into a trusted leader in healthcare innovation. Since 2010, we’ve been guided by compassion, innovation, and research-driven care.


Our founding team outside the first Eloheh Health building. 		In the early days, Eloheh Health was nothing more than three friends with an idea. They saw communities struggling to access affordable healthcare and believed technology could change that. Partnership with patients and providers was the foundation of our approach.

Company Timeline

  1. 2010 — Founded by three Purdue alumni with a shared vision.
  2. 2013 — Released our first biomedical device for remote care.
  3. 2017 — Expanded into telehealth services across the Midwest.
  4. 2020 — Partnered with hospitals during COVID-19 to support patients at home.
  5. 2022 — Formed nationwide clinical partnerships and research collaborations.

Our Core Values

Sustainability — Lasting healthcare solutions. Innovation — Technology improving lives.
Partnership — Strong ties with providers and patients. Compassion — Human-centered care above all.
“Eloheh Health was built on the belief that small changes in access can make big differences in people’s lives.”
— Maria Gonzalez, Co-Founder

Eloheh Health Today

Today, Eloheh Health serves patients nationwide with telehealth, preventive care, and biomedical devices. Our journey proves that innovation paired with compassion can transform communities. Looking forward, we remain dedicated to research and building healthier futures for all.
