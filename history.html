Our Story & History

From pop-up screenings to a connected-care platform.

Eloheh Health started in 2015 running campus wellness pop-ups. We focused on prevention and early detection with community partners. By 2019 we expanded screening to employers; in 2022 we launched our remote monitoring service.

Milestones

  1. 2015 — First pop-up screenings with local nurses
  2. 2019 — Employer wellness and on-site clinics
  3. 2022 — Remote monitoring + outcome dashboards

Values we live by

  • Patient dignity and informed choice
  • Evidence-based protocols and safety
  • Local partnerships and access
