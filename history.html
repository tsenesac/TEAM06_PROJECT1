Eloheh Health

“Caring for you, innovating for tomorrow”

Welcome! This page tells the story of how Eloheh Health grew from a small local clinic into a trusted leader in healthcare innovation. Since 2010, we’ve been guided by a commitment to better health outcomes, advanced technology, and compassionate care.


Our founding team outside the first Eloheh Health building.

Company Timeline

  1. 2010 — Founded by three Purdue alumni with a vision for accessible healthcare.
  2. 2013 — Released our first biomedical device to assist in remote patient monitoring.
  3. 2017 — Expanded into telehealth and virtual care services across the Midwest.
  4. 2020 — Partnered with regional hospitals during the COVID-19 pandemic to expand remote care.
  5. 2022 — Formed nationwide clinical partnerships and broadened research efforts.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability — Building healthcare systems that last for future generations.
  • Innovation — Using technology to improve the lives of patients worldwide.
  • Partnership — Working hand-in-hand with doctors, nurses, and patients.
  • Compassion — Never losing sight of the human side of healthcare.

The Early Years

Eloheh Health began as a community initiative, where three graduates saw families struggling to access care. With limited resources, they combined research knowledge and grassroots connections to provide practical solutions. Their vision was simple: create technology that makes care affordable, accessible, and effective.

Eloheh Health Today

Today, Eloheh Health is proud to serve patients nationwide with telehealth, preventive care, and advanced biomedical devices. Our journey proves that innovation paired with compassion can transform entire communities. Looking forward, we remain dedicated to research and to building healthier futures for all.
