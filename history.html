Coltivare — Company Story & History

Welcome! This page shares how Coltivare started, our values, and the milestones that shaped us.

Founding Timeline

  1. 2010 — Coltivare is founded by three Purdue agriculture grads in a small community co-op.
  2. 2013 — Launched our first sustainable fertilizer product that reduced runoff by 40%.
  3. 2017 — Expanded distribution across the Midwest.
  4. 2022 — Began offering farm-management consulting services.

Core Values

  • Sustainability — caring for land and community.
  • Innovation — practical tech for everyday farms.
  • Partnership — we grow with our customers.

Our Early Story

What started as a local seed-exchange among neighbors quickly turned into a small business. The founders — Maria Gonzalez, Lee Thompson, and Priya Nair — shared a vision: better yields, healthier soil.

By focusing on research and farmer feedback, Coltivare became known for practical solutions that worked in real fields. Today we still visit farms weekly to learn directly from growers.
